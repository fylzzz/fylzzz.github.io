Description

Riffs of Resistance was produced by a team of 2nd and 3rd year Griffith University Game Design and Production students for Games Studio 2025.
The team was tasked with creating a rhythm-based action game inspired by the likes of Hi-Fi Rush, Devil May Cry series, etc.

Individual Contribution

I joined the team in the second half of development as Audio Lead. In this position, I was responsible for the design and implementation of all audio assets.
I also assisted the programming team in working with audio middleware to design rhythm-based gameplay elements.

Deliverables:

4 music tracks

Wrote and recorded looping tracks for 3 scenes (Title, Tutorial and Level 1,) with consideration for dynamic gameplay situations
 Implemented tracks in engine utilising audio middleware (FMOD)

24+ SFX

Designed unique SFX for all interactions in-game

FMOD Unity API custom framework for accessing music data

Implemented dynamic music tracks that adjust to gameplay (in/out of combat)
 Implemented music playback tracking for syncing gameplay to music
 Worked closely with programming team to implement rhythm-based combat and environmental features

Conducted both internal and external playtests to gain user feedback on gameplay features and identify issues/bugs
